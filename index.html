ABOUT ME

A Bachelor’s graduate of Wentworth Institute of Technology, with a foundation rooted in architecture and urbanism, informed by advanced design thinking and technological exploration.


Professional experience includes two official internship semesters (May 2023–August 2023 and January 2024–April 2024), alongside an additional voluntary internship from August 2022 to September 2022, undertaken to extend practical and technical depth beyond academic requirements.


A Study Abroad program at Berlin International University expanded architectural and urban perspectives through exposure to international design culture, experimental methodologies, and contemporary spatial discourse.


Guided by fluid geometries and urban dynamics, the work engages architecture and urbanism as interconnected systems, exploring how form, movement, and technology shape spatial continuity from building to city scale.
